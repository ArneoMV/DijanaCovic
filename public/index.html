I paint with oil, acrylic and watercolors usually on cotton or linen canvas.

I am painting mostly in the manner of abstract realism, contrasting two seemingly incompatible concepts, abstract art and realistic art. Abstract art does not refer to real objects. By abstract use of a lines, shapes, colors, texture and pattern, I create movement, give rhythm, evoke emotions. Realism tries to capture moments from real life and time or the personality of individuals or nature that resemble real life.

Looking for a balance of values ​​of size and shape

Focused on design elements and principles to visually express feelings, thoughts and my own experiences. Focusing to create a balance of values ​​and sizes or shapes and lines. Painting is like beautiful music to me, if I were to put myself in genres, my painting is pure, good, soul and blues.
