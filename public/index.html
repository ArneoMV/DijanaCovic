Hi, I'm Diana - fine art painter and journalist.

Contemporary oil painter and TV journalist from Croatia. On this page, you can have a glance at her art and get info regarding further information and the potential purchase of her works.

Explore paintings

She often draws inspiration from people and situations surrounding them. Apart from that, nature often inspires her as a source of pure beauty of colors and images. Especially in spring, which is a frequent theme of floral motifs that exude strong color.

Style:

Surrealism, figurative, portraiture, impressionism, modern, pop art

Technique:

Oil, gesso, acrylic, ink, watercolor, gouache, charcoal

Dimensions:

70x50, 80x60, 90x60, 120x80 centimeters

Technique
instinctive, intuitive, and full of emotion.

The movements of the brush and spatulas are rhythmic, energetic, and strong like a dance, with thick gestural layers of color. Her work is often characterized by impressionist poses, often with some contemporary surreal detail and a strong message. She often uses erasing paint with solvents and applies wide traces of oil coating to evoke the realism of figures and portraits and to enhance their expression. Spatulas evoke the abstraction of shapes, lines and enhance the rhythmicity of the image.

Her work
Balance of visual expression and personal influences.

Bursting with decoration and aesthetics, carries a strong influence of older masters but a dash of more contemporary artists is by no means to be ignored. Her passion for aesthetic appeal is a real “force of joy”. Images of nature in her hands become experiences.

I mostly paint on canvas, but I’m no stranger to drawing on other objects.
